Penksza Department of Botany and Plant Physiology, Agricultural University, H-2103 , Gödöllö Hungary Abstract: The eastern part of the Gerecse Mountains, Hungary, is floristically much less known than the other parts of the country. A small area at Bajna has contributed with 11 new records to the flora of the mountains. Floristic data, collected by the author since 1984 on the Or Hill, near Bajna, Gerecse Moun- tains, Hungary, are presented. The list of taxa was completed with literature data (Feichtinger 1865, 1899 and Boros 1938, 1952). The species list given here supplements former results of Seregélyes ( 1977) and Szolláth (1980), published on the Gerecse Mountains (a territory with a code of 8377 in the Central European flora cata- logue). In the list, however, the presence of some species is published for the first time about the Gerecse Mountains. Feichtinger mentions some species the pres- ence of which is not confirmed by this study. Some of the specimens of these species is found in the herbarium of the Botanical Section of the Natural History Museum, Budapest. These are listed below, together with the date of collection and herbarium sheet-numbers. Trigonella monspeliaca VI.1866. 96312, 96302, 96319, 297471 Malcolmia africana V. 1858. 318746 Teucrium botrys V. 1860. 140429 Cerastium brachypetalum VI. 1858. 340608 Valerianella cannata V. 1860. 159275, 159277 Taxa not deposited in the herbarium include: Carex supina Festuca drymeia , Ornitho galium refractum} Myosotis silvatica, Peucedanum are- narium and Thesium bav arum. Species reported by Boros but not recorded by the author are: Verbascum speciosum (VI. 21. 1938.) Geranium rotundifolium (VI. 21. 1938.) Hieracium pallidum (VI. 21. 1938.) Myosotis stricta (IV. 20. 1952.) Future investigations are necessary to estab- lish whether these species have become extinct from the study area.
